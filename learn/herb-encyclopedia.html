Aloe Vera

Scientific Name: Aloe barbadensis miller

Description: Aloe Vera is a succulent plant known for its fleshy, gel-filled leaves. It is widely used in Ayurveda for its cooling, soothing, and moisturizing properties.

Uses:

  • Aloe Vera gel is applied topically to treat burns, cuts, and skin irritations.
  • It is also consumed to aid digestion and improve gut health.

Verified by AYUSH: Yes

Amla (Indian Gooseberry)

Scientific Name: Phyllanthus emblica

Description: Amla is a small tree that produces greenish-yellow, sour fruits packed with Vitamin C. In Ayurveda, Amla is considered a powerful rejuvenator.

Uses:

  • Amla boosts immunity and improves skin and hair health.
  • It helps in digestion and detoxifies the liver.

Verified by AYUSH: Yes

Turmeric
